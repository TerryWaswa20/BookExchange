Our Services

Thienry Book Exchange provides an easy way to exchange revision books with different people around and even far from you, hire revision books from people and organizations and buy new books from our best bookshops.

How

Register to have your account and be able to see other people who have posted books for exchange and hire.

Exchange

Exchange enables you to exchange your used revision books with another person who is in need of them and has other revision books you need.

Hire

We give you an opportunity to have revision books from other people or an organization at a fee and for a period of time.

Buy

We link you with the best bookshops to purchase new revision books for your kid at a friendly costs.
